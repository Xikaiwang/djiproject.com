Introduction to the CEO

Hello to all the visitors that are dedicated to DJI products, this is Blake Wang, I am the Chief Executive Officer of DJI. I am so pleased to introduce myself as a “spectre” of this amazing company, I have experienced a tough time establishing this business, whereas all my consistent effort eventually comes up with a promising result. My group and I were so addicted to making the perfect consuming level product that allows everybody to enjoy flying drones and photography.

Innovation is the only resource of making progress in our organisation, we are trying to run the company for profits and corporate social responsibility simultaneously. Some reactions that have already revealed our CSR, we are consistent in not using our products for war or turmoil. We have been using our products for agriculture, cultivation, in-air photography and education. Moreover, we have employed many groups of disability employees. They were part of our organisation and play an important role in project implementing, idea ripping, and product designing.

At first glance, I fell in love with it; the perfectly contoured design and the impressive inverted seagull wing design were deeply embedded in my mind. My first experience of flying my electric plane remains an unforgettable memory for me as I experienced dozens of failures before it was broadcast. I think many children with the same dream would find it difficult to achieve this goal and would not have enough time and money to achieve an independently designed plane in the sky.
